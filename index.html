Addressing Key Challenges

Challenge: Data Quality & Misinformation

Mitigation: Our multi-pronged approach of Dynamic Trust Scoring, AI media verification, and a final manual admin review process ensures a high degree of data reliability.

Challenge: Social Media API Limitations

Mitigation: We will employ intelligent, keyword-focused data fetching and robust caching mechanisms to optimize API usage, manage costs, and stay within rate limits.

Challenge: User Adoption in Remote Communities

Mitigation: The app will be gamified, offered in multiple regional languages, and promoted through partnerships with local authorities to build trust and drive adoption.
